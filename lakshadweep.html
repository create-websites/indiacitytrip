Top 20 Places to Visit in Lakshadweep


  1. Agatti Island - Famous for its turquoise lagoons and water sports. Best time to visit is October to May.

  2. Kavaratti Island - The capital of Lakshadweep, known for its marine aquarium and scenic beauty. Best time to visit is October to March.

  3. Bangaram Atoll - A secluded paradise with pristine beaches and coral reefs. Best time to visit is October to March.

  4. Minicoy Island - Home to a historic lighthouse and rich culture. Best time to visit is October to May.

  5. Kalpeni Island - Known for its scenic lagoons and kayaking activities. Best time to visit is October to March.

  6. Kadmat Island - A haven for marine biodiversity and diving experiences. Best time to visit is October to March.

  7. Thinnakara Island - A serene getaway perfect for relaxation. Best time to visit is October to March.

  8. Pitti Island - A bird sanctuary with a variety of seabirds. Best time to visit is October to March.

  9. Suheli Par - A remote atoll with stunning marine life. Best time to visit is October to May.

  10. Andrott Island - Rich in Buddhist history and local culture. Best time to visit is October to March.

  11. Chetlat Island - Known for its sandy beaches and fishing activities. Best time to visit is October to March.

  12. Bitra Island - The smallest inhabited island with an enchanting landscape. Best time to visit is October to March.

  13. Amini Island - Famous for its coral formations and scenic views. Best time to visit is October to March.

  14. Kiltan Island - A remote island known for its lagoons and fishing culture. Best time to visit is October to March.

  15. Parali Island - A small but picturesque island for nature lovers. Best time to visit is October to March.

  16. Viringili Island - Ideal for a peaceful and secluded getaway. Best time to visit is October to March.

  17. Perumal Par - A unique spot for adventurous divers. Best time to visit is October to March.

  18. Kalpitti Island - A perfect spot for birdwatching and relaxation. Best time to visit is October to March.

  19. Pitti Bank - A great location for snorkeling and diving enthusiasts. Best time to visit is October to March.
