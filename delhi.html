Top 20 Places to Visit in Delhi


  1. India Gate - Iconic war memorial and popular picnic spot. Best time to visit is during the evening year-round.

  2. Red Fort - Historic fort with a light and sound show. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  3. Qutub Minar - A UNESCO World Heritage Site and architectural marvel. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  4. Lotus Temple - A serene Bahai's House of Worship. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  5. Akshardham Temple - Stunning temple with water and light shows. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  6. Humayun's Tomb - A beautiful Mughal garden-tomb. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  7. Chandni Chowk - Bustling market famous for street food and shopping. Best time to visit is during the cooler months (October-March).

  8. Jama Masjid - One of the largest mosques in India. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  9. Raj Ghat - Memorial dedicated to Mahatma Gandhi. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  10. National Museum - A treasure trove of India's history and culture. Best time to visit is year-round.

  11. Rashtrapati Bhavan - Official residence of the President of India. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  12. Lodhi Gardens - A serene park perfect for a peaceful stroll. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  13. Gurudwara Bangla Sahib - A prominent Sikh shrine with a peaceful ambiance. Best time to visit is year-round.

  14. Delhi Haat - A cultural and shopping hub offering traditional crafts and cuisines. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  15. Garden of Five Senses - A picturesque park ideal for relaxation. Best time to visit is during the spring and winter seasons.

  16. Purana Qila - A historic fort with a boating lake. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  17. Tughlaqabad Fort - A ruined fort with historical significance. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  18. National Rail Museum - A museum showcasing India's railway heritage. Best time to visit is year-round.

  19. Nehru Planetarium - A center for astronomy enthusiasts. Best time to visit is year-round.

  20. Crafts Museum - A museum showcasing traditional Indian crafts. Best time to visit is year-round.
