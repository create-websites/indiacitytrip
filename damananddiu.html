Top 20 Places to See in Diu and Daman


  1. Diu Fort - A historic fort with breathtaking sea views. Best time to visit is from October to February.

  2. Nagoa Beach - A serene beach perfect for relaxation and swimming. Best time to visit is from October to February.

  3. Jampore Beach - Known for its tranquility and stunning sunsets. Best time to visit is from November to February.

  4. Moti Daman Fort - A magnificent fort showcasing colonial architecture. Best time to visit is from October to March.

  5. Devka Beach - A family-friendly beach with scenic views. Best time to visit is from October to March.

  6. Chakratirth Beach - A tranquil beach offering picturesque landscapes. Best time to visit is from November to February.

  7. Ghoghla Beach - Known for its golden sands and calm waters. Best time to visit is from October to February.

  8. Zampa Gateway - A historic gateway adorned with carvings. Best time to visit is from November to February.

  9. INS Khukri Memorial - A naval memorial honoring brave martyrs. Best time to visit is from October to March.

  10. Naida Caves - Unique rock formations offering a surreal experience. Best time to visit is from November to February.

  11. Church of Bom Jesus - A stunning church showcasing Portuguese architecture. Best time to visit is from October to February.

  12. Somnath Mahadev Temple - A serene temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. Best time to visit is from October to March.

  13. Panikotha - A picturesque fort accessible by boat. Best time to visit is from November to February.

  14. Sea Shell Museum - A unique museum showcasing a collection of sea shells. Best time to visit is from October to March.

  15. Nani Daman - A charming area with colonial buildings and churches. Best time to visit is from October to February.

  16. Jetty Garden - A relaxing garden with views of the Daman Ganga River. Best time to visit is from November to February.

  17. Light House - Offers panoramic views of the Arabian Sea. Best time to visit is from November to February.

  18. Bhenslore Beach - A lesser-known gem with serene surroundings. Best time to visit is from October to February.

  19. St. Jerome Fort - A historic fort located on the banks of the Daman Ganga River. Best time to visit is from October to February.
