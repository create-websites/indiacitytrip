Top 20 Cities to See in Haryana


  1. Kurukshetra - Known for Brahma Sarovar, Jyotisar, and historical significance. Best time to visit is October-March.

  2. Gurugram - Famous for Kingdom of Dreams, Cyber Hub, and modern architecture. Best time to visit is October-March.

  3. Panipat - Historical city known for Panipat War Memorial and Kabuli Bagh Mosque. Best time to visit is October-March.

  4. Faridabad - Known for Surajkund International Crafts Mela and Badkhal Lake. Best time to visit is February for the fair.

  5. Hisar - Known for Firoz Shah Palace Complex and Haryana Agricultural University. Best time to visit is October-March.

  6. Ambala - Famous for its cloth market, Bhawani Amba Temple, and Gurudwara Manji Sahib. Best time to visit is October-March.

  7. Karnal - Known for Karnal Lake and historic monuments. Best time to visit is October-March.

  8. Rewari - Known for the Heritage Steam Locomotive Museum and local crafts. Best time to visit is October-March.

  9. Sultanpur - Famous for Sultanpur National Park, a paradise for bird watchers. Best time to visit is November-February.

  10. Rohtak - Known for Tilyar Lake and the historic Rohtak Fort. Best time to visit is October-March.

  11. Yamunanagar - Famous for Kalesar National Park and scenic riverside locations. Best time to visit is October-March.

  12. Panchkula - Known for Pinjore Gardens and Morni Hills. Best time to visit is October-March.

  13. Kaithal - Rich in history with attractions like Kapil Muni Temple and Kalayat Temple. Best time to visit is October-March.

  14. Bhiwani - Known for temples and being a center for boxing. Best time to visit is October-March.

  15. Jind - Famous for Jayanti Devi Temple and its historical significance. Best time to visit is October-March.

  16. Mahendragarh - Known for Dhosi Hill and its ancient heritage. Best time to visit is October-March.

  17. Palwal - Known for Gandhi Seva Ashram and its rich history. Best time to visit is October-March.

  18. Fatehabad - Known for Banawali and Bhuna archaeological sites. Best time to visit is October-March.

  19. Jhajjar - Famous for Bhindawas Wildlife Sanctuary and Pandu Pol Temple. Best time to visit is November-February.

  20. Sirsa - Known for Ghaggar River and ancient religious sites. Best time to visit is October-March.
