Top 20 Places to Visit in Puducherry


  1. Promenade Beach - A beautiful beachfront ideal for a morning walk. Best time to visit is October to March.

  2. Auroville - A spiritual township promoting peace and harmony. Best time to visit is year-round.

  3. French Quarter - A historic district showcasing French colonial architecture. Best time to visit is October to March.

  4. Paradise Beach - A pristine beach accessible by boat. Best time to visit is October to March.

  5. Arulmigu Manakula Vinayagar Temple - A famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha. Best time to visit is year-round.

  6. Basilica of the Sacred Heart of Jesus - A stunning Catholic church with Gothic architecture. Best time to visit is year-round.

  7. Puducherry Museum - Showcasing artifacts from the French colonial period. Best time to visit is year-round.

  8. Botanical Garden - A lush green park with a variety of plant species. Best time to visit is October to March.

  9. Chunnambar Boat House - A scenic boating destination near Paradise Beach. Best time to visit is October to March.

  10. Serenity Beach - A peaceful and lesser-crowded beach ideal for surfing. Best time to visit is October to March.

  11. Ousteri Lake - A picturesque freshwater lake and bird sanctuary. Best time to visit is October to March.

  12. Goubert Avenue - A scenic road along the coast, perfect for an evening stroll. Best time to visit is year-round.

  13. Immaculate Conception Cathedral - A 300-year-old church with colonial charm. Best time to visit is year-round.

  14. Veerampattinam Beach - The longest beach in Puducherry, known for its fishing village. Best time to visit is October to March.

  15. Jawahar Toy Museum - A delightful place showcasing a collection of dolls and toys. Best time to visit is year-round.

  16. Sri Aurobindo Ashram - A place of meditation and spirituality. Best time to visit is year-round.

  17. Bharathi Park - A lush garden in the heart of the city, great for relaxation. Best time to visit is October to March.

  18. Ananda Ranga Pillai Mansion - A historical house reflecting Indo-French architecture. Best time to visit is year-round.

  19. Old Lighthouse - A landmark offering stunning coastal views. Best time to visit is year-round.
