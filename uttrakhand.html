Top 20 Cities to See in Uttarakhand


  1. Dehradun - Known for its scenic landscapes and proximity to the Rajaji National Park. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-February).

  2. Haridwar - A significant pilgrimage city, Haridwar is known for the Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-February).

  3. Rishikesh - Famous for yoga and adventure activities like rafting, Rishikesh is a spiritual haven. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-February).

  4. Nainital - Known for Naini Lake, Nainital is a picturesque hill station offering boating and nature walks. Best time to visit is during the summer season (March-June).

  5. Mussoorie - Famous for its colonial architecture and stunning views of the Himalayas. Best time to visit is during the summer season (March-June).

  6. Kausani - Known for its panoramic views of the snow-capped peaks, Kausani is perfect for a peaceful retreat. Best time to visit is during the summer season (March-June).

  7. Almora - A charming town with rich cultural heritage, Almora is famous for its temples and scenic beauty. Best time to visit is during the summer season (March-June).

  8. Jim Corbett National Park - Known for its wildlife safaris, Corbett is a great destination for nature lovers. Best time to visit is during the winter season (November-February).

  9. Ranikhet - Known for its scenic beauty and the army cantonment, Ranikhet offers a peaceful retreat. Best time to visit is during the summer season (March-June).

  10. Harshil - A beautiful village near Gangotri, Harshil is famous for its apple orchards and panoramic views. Best time to visit is during the summer season (March-June).

  11. Tehri - Known for the Tehri Dam and lake, Tehri offers a variety of adventure sports. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-February).

  12. Chopta - Known for its trekking routes and stunning views of the Himalayan peaks, Chopta is often referred to as the "Mini Switzerland of India". Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-February).

  13. Uttarkashi - Known as the "Kashi of the North", Uttarkashi is a religious and cultural destination. Best time to visit is during the summer season (March-June).

  14. Haldwani - Known as the gateway to Nainital, Haldwani offers a blend of nature and commerce. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-February).

  15. Pauri - A serene hill station offering picturesque views of the Himalayan ranges, Pauri is known for its natural beauty. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-February).

  16. Binsar - Known for the Binsar Wildlife Sanctuary, Binsar offers beautiful views of the surrounding mountains. Best time to visit is during the summer season (March-June).

  17. Khirsu - A lesser-known destination, Khirsu is known for its peaceful ambiance and apple orchards. Best time to visit is during the summer season (March-June).

  18. Sat Tal - Known for its seven interconnected lakes, Sat Tal is a beautiful location for boating and nature walks. Best time to visit is during the summer season (March-June).

  19. Gopeshwar - A small town known for its temples and trekking routes, Gopeshwar is a peaceful retreat. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-February).
