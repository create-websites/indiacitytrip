Top 20 Places to See in Sikkim


  1. Gangtok - The capital city, known for its vibrant culture and Rumtek Monastery. Best time to visit is from October to March.

  2. Yumthang Valley - Famous for its stunning valley views and serene environment. Best time to visit is from February to June and September to November.

  3. Pelling - Known for Pemayangtse Monastery and breathtaking views of Mount Kanchenjunga. Best time to visit is from March to May and September to December.

  4. Ravangla - A beautiful hill station known for Buddha Park and scenic landscapes. Best time to visit is from March to May and September to November.

  5. Tsomgo Lake - A glacial lake situated at a high altitude, known for its scenic beauty. Best time to visit is from April to May and October to November.

  6. Lachung - Known for its picturesque landscapes and Yumthang Valley. Best time to visit is from October to June.

  7. Zuluk - Famous for its stunning views and winding roads. Best time to visit is from August to September and November to February.

  8. Nathula Pass - A mountain pass offering breathtaking views and historical significance. Best time to visit is from May to October.

  9. Namchi - Known for its panoramic views and the Namchi Monastery. Best time to visit is from March to May and October to November.

  10. Rumtek Monastery - A significant Buddhist monastery known for its architecture and spiritual ambiance. Best time to visit is from October to December.

  11. Khangchendzonga National Park - Known for its rich biodiversity and scenic beauty. Best time to visit is from March to May and September to November.

  12. Yuksom - A historic town known for its monasteries and trekking routes. Best time to visit is from March to June and September to November.

  13. Gurudongmar Lake - One of the highest lakes in the world, known for its stunning beauty. Best time to visit is from November to June.

  14. Goecha La - A popular trekking destination offering breathtaking views of the Himalayas. Best time to visit is from April to June and September to November.

  15. Silk Route - A historic trade route offering stunning views and scenic drives. Best time to visit is from April to June and September to November.

  16. Temi Tea Garden - The only tea estate in Sikkim, known for its picturesque landscape. Best time to visit is from March to May and September to November.

  17. Kaluk - A serene village known for its natural beauty and trekking routes. Best time to visit is from March to May and September to November.

  18. Phodong Monastery - A significant Buddhist monastery known for its beautiful architecture. Best time to visit is from March to May and October to December.

  19. Rinchenpong - A peaceful village offering stunning views and trekking opportunities. Best time to visit is from March to May and September to November.

  20. Singalila National Park - Known for its rich biodiversity and trekking routes. Best time to visit is from March to May and September to November.
