Top 20 Cities to See in Arunachal Pradesh


  1. Itanagar - Known for Itanagar Fort and Ganga Lake, showcasing the rich cultural heritage of the state. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  2. Tawang - Famous for Tawang Monastery and scenic Himalayan views. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  3. Ziro - Renowned for its lush landscapes, Apatani culture, and vibrant festivals. Best time to visit is during the monsoon season (July-September).

  4. Pasighat - Known as the "Gateway to Arunachal", offering rafting and scenic beauty. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  5. Bomdila - Famous for Bomdila Monastery and panoramic Himalayan views. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  6. Dirang - Known for its hot springs, orchards, and Dirang Dzong fortress. Best time to visit is during the spring season (March-May).

  7. Tezu - Famous for Parasuram Kund and its scenic landscapes. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  8. Roing - Known for its Mehao Lake and ancient forts. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  9. Changlang - Famous for Namdapha National Park and diverse tribal culture. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  10. Along - Known for its orange orchards and tribal festivals. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  11. Yingkiong - Known for its serene beauty and trekking opportunities. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  12. Khonsa - Famous for its rich biodiversity and tribal villages. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  13. Seppa - Known for its picturesque surroundings and serene environment. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  14. Changlang - Famous for its stunning views and unique tribal heritage. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  15. Mechuka - Known for its unexplored beauty and Buddhist monasteries. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  16. Hayuliang - Known for its breathtaking landscapes and tranquil surroundings. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  17. Deomali - Famous for its scenic beauty and trekking spots. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  18. Daporijo - Known for its picturesque beauty and vibrant tribal culture. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  19. Walong - Famous for its historic significance and serene beauty. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  20. Jairampur - Known for its rich cultural heritage and historic World War II cemetery. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).
