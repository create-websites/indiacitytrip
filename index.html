Top 20 Cities to See in India


  1. New Delhi - The capital city of India, New Delhi is known for its rich history, culture, and landmarks such as the Red Fort, India Gate, and Lotus Temple. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-February) when the weather is cool and pleasant.

  2. Mumbai, Maharashtra - The financial capital of India, Mumbai is famous for its Bollywood industry, Gateway of India, and vibrant nightlife. Best time to visit is during the winter season (November-February) when the weather is mild.

  3. Jaipur, Rajasthan - Known as the "Pink City", Jaipur is famous for its forts, palaces, and colorful bazaars. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-February) when the weather is cooler.

  4. Agra, Uttar Pradesh - Home to the Taj Mahal, Agra is a must-visit city for history lovers. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March) when the weather is pleasant for sightseeing.

  5. Bengaluru, Karnataka - Known as the "Silicon Valley of India", Bengaluru is famous for its tech industry, parks, and breweries. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-February) when the weather is mild.

  6. Chennai, Tamil Nadu - Known for its beaches, temples, and rich cultural heritage, Chennai is a vibrant city. Best time to visit is during the winter season (November-February) when the weather is cooler.

  7. Kolkata, West Bengal - Known for its colonial architecture, cultural festivals, and historic landmarks, Kolkata is a city with a rich cultural heritage. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-February) when the weather is pleasant.

  8. Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh - One of the oldest living cities in the world, Varanasi is known for its spiritual significance, ghats, and temples. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-February) when the weather is cooler.

  9. Udaipur, Rajasthan - Known as the "City of Lakes", Udaipur is famous for its palaces, lakes, and temples. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-February) when the weather is mild and pleasant.

  10. Rishikesh, Uttarakhand - Known for its yoga retreats, river rafting, and scenic beauty, Rishikesh is a popular destination for adventure seekers and spiritual travelers. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-February) when the weather is cool.

  11. Amritsar, Punjab - Known for the Golden Temple, Amritsar is a city with religious and historical significance. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March) when the weather is pleasant.

  12. Pushkar, Rajasthan - Famous for its annual camel fair, temples, and beautiful lake, Pushkar is a charming city to visit. Best time to visit is during the winter season (November-February) when the weather is cooler.

  13. Hyderabad, Telangana - Known for its rich history, monuments, and the famous Hyderabadi biryani, Hyderabad is a bustling metropolis. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-February) when the weather is mild.

  14. Kerala (Alleppey & Kochi) - Famous for its backwaters, beaches, and Kerala cuisine, Kerala is a tropical paradise. Best time to visit is during the winter season (November-February) when the weather is pleasant.

  15. Goa - Known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage, Goa is a popular destination for tourists. Best time to visit is during the winter season (November-February) when the weather is perfect for beach activities.

  16. Jaipur, Rajasthan - Known for its forts, palaces, and vibrant markets, Jaipur is a must-visit city for history enthusiasts. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-February) when the weather is pleasant.

  17. Darjeeling, West Bengal - Known for its tea gardens, breathtaking views of the Himalayas, and the famous Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, this hill station is a must-visit. Best time to visit is during the summer months (April-June).

  18. Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh - Known for its Mughal architecture, kebabs, and cultural heritage, Lucknow offers a unique blend of history and cuisine. Best time to visit is during the winter season (November-February) when the weather is cool.

  19. Ooty, Tamil Nadu - Known as the "Queen of Hill Stations", Ooty offers stunning views of the Nilgiri Hills, gardens, and lakes. Best time to visit is during the summer months (April-June) when the weather is pleasant.

  20. Shimla, Himachal Pradesh - Known for its scenic beauty and colonial architecture, Shimla is a popular hill station. Best time to visit is during the summer season (March-June) when the weather is mild and pleasant.
