Top 20 Places to See in Goa


  1. Panaji - The capital city, known for its churches, Portuguese architecture, and Fontainhas. Best time to visit is from November to March.

  2. Calangute - Known as the "Queen of Beaches", Calangute is famous for its water sports and vibrant nightlife. Best time to visit is from October to March.

  3. Baga - Popular for beach parties, shacks, and nightlife. Best time to visit is from November to February.

  4. Vasco da Gama - Known for its natural beauty and naval history. Best time to visit is from October to March.

  5. Anjuna - Famous for its flea market, beach parties, and laid-back vibe. Best time to visit is from October to March.

  6. Chapora - Known for Chapora Fort and breathtaking views of Vagator Beach. Best time to visit is from November to February.

  7. Palolem - A serene beach perfect for relaxation and dolphin spotting. Best time to visit is from November to March.

  8. Colva - Known for its long sandy beach and vibrant nightlife. Best time to visit is from October to March.

  9. Margao - Famous for its Portuguese heritage and Old Goa charm. Best time to visit is from November to February.

  10. Aguada - Known for Aguada Fort and stunning views of the Arabian Sea. Best time to visit is from October to March.

  11. Miramar - A clean and beautiful beach close to Panaji. Best time to visit is from November to March.

  12. Mapusa - Known for its Friday market and cultural vibrancy. Best time to visit is from October to February.

  13. Arambol - A bohemian haven, famous for its beaches and drum circles. Best time to visit is from November to February.

  14. Mollem - Home to the Dudhsagar Falls and Bhagwan Mahavir Wildlife Sanctuary. Best time to visit is from October to February.

  15. Vagator - Famous for its rocky cliffs, shacks, and scenic beauty. Best time to visit is from October to March.

  16. Betalbatim - Known for its peaceful beach and fishing charm. Best time to visit is from November to February.

  17. Salcete - A culturally rich area with beautiful beaches and churches. Best time to visit is from November to February.

  18. Old Goa - Famous for its UNESCO World Heritage Churches. Best time to visit is from October to February.

  19. Canacona - Known for its serene beaches, Palolem and Agonda. Best time to visit is from November to March.
