Top 20 Cities to See in Manipur


  1. Imphal - The capital city known for Kangla Fort, Ima Keithel (women's market), and Loktak Lake. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  2. Moirang - Famous for its historical significance, Loktak Lake, and Keibul Lamjao National Park. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  3. Ukhrul - Known for Shirui Lily, Khayang Peak, and trekking opportunities. Best time to visit is during October-June.

  4. Churachandpur - A hub for tribal culture and picturesque landscapes. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  5. Thoubal - Renowned for its serene atmosphere, lush greenery, and agricultural significance. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  6. Bishnupur - Famous for its ancient temples, including the iconic Vishnu Temple. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  7. Kakching - Known for its beautiful parks, cleanliness, and cultural richness. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  8. Senapati - A nature lover's paradise with scenic landscapes and vibrant tribal culture. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  9. Moreh - A bustling border town, gateway to Myanmar, and a hub for trade and tourism. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  10. Noney - Renowned for its untouched natural beauty, waterfalls, and hiking trails. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  11. Jiribam - Known for its unique mix of cultures and serene environment. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  12. Tengnoupal - Offers breathtaking views of the mountains and valleys. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  13. Chandel - Rich in tribal heritage and home to beautiful landscapes. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  14. Andro - Known for its pottery, cultural village, and scenic surroundings. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  15. Kangpokpi - A serene destination with unexplored beauty and rich culture. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  16. Tamenglong - Known as the "Land of Hornbills," with majestic waterfalls and exotic flora. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  17. Mayang Imphal - Famous for its traditional boat racing and rich cultural heritage. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  18. Waithou - Renowned for its beautiful lake and pineapple farming. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  19. Khongjom - A historically significant site known for the Anglo-Manipuri War memorial. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  20. Thanga - A picturesque location on the Loktak Lake with mesmerizing views. Best time to visit is during October-March.
