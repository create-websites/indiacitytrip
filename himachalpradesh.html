Top 20 Cities to See in Himachal Pradesh


  1. Shimla - Known as the "Queen of Hills", famous for Mall Road, Christ Church, and Jakhoo Temple. Best time to visit is March-June and October-November.

  2. Manali - Known for its scenic beauty, Solang Valley, and Rohtang Pass. Best time to visit is October-June.

  3. Dharamshala - Known for the Dalai Lama's residence and monasteries. Best time to visit is March-June.

  4. Dalhousie - Known for its colonial charm, Khajjiar (Mini Switzerland), and lush landscapes. Best time to visit is March-June.

  5. Kullu - Famous for its valleys, trekking routes, and Beas River. Best time to visit is October-June.

  6. Spiti Valley - Known for its remote beauty, monasteries, and adventure activities. Best time to visit is May-October.

  7. Kasol - Popular for its hippie vibes, trekking routes, and Parvati Valley. Best time to visit is October-May.

  8. Chamba - Famous for its temples, Suhi Mata Mela, and Ravi River. Best time to visit is March-June.

  9. Kinnaur - Known for its apple orchards, Sangla Valley, and Kalpa. Best time to visit is April-October.

  10. Bir - Famous for paragliding and Tibetan monasteries. Best time to visit is October-May.

  11. McLeod Ganj - Known for its Tibetan culture, Dal Lake, and Triund Trek. Best time to visit is March-June.

  12. Barot - Known for its serene beauty, Uhl River, and trout fishing. Best time to visit is March-June and October-November.

  13. Palampur - Famous for its tea gardens, Bundla Stream, and temples. Best time to visit is March-June.

  14. Keylong - Known for its monasteries and adventure activities. Best time to visit is May-October.

  15. Kufri - A favorite for skiing and snow adventures. Best time to visit is December-February.

  16. Hamirpur - Known for its temples and serene environment. Best time to visit is October-June.

  17. Narkanda - Famous for its ski slopes and Hatu Peak. Best time to visit is December-March for skiing.

  18. Sirmaur - Known for its Renuka Lake and temples. Best time to visit is March-June and October-November.

  19. Tirthan Valley - Popular for its riverside beauty and the Great Himalayan National Park. Best time to visit is March-June.

  20. Solan - Known for its Shoolini Temple and monasteries. Best time to visit is October-June.
