Top 20 Cities to See in Rajasthan


  1. Jaipur - Known as the "Pink City", Jaipur is famous for its Amer Fort, Hawa Mahal, and vibrant bazaars. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  2. Udaipur - Known as the "City of Lakes", Udaipur boasts the stunning Lake Pichola, City Palace, and Jag Mandir. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  3. Jaisalmer - Known as the "Golden City", Jaisalmer is famous for its fort, sand dunes, and cultural festivals. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  4. Jodhpur - Known as the "Blue City", Jodhpur is home to the Mehrangarh Fort, Umaid Bhawan Palace, and Clock Tower. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  5. Bikaner - Known for its Junagarh Fort, camel festival, and Karni Mata Temple. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  6. Pushkar - Famous for its Brahma Temple, Pushkar Lake, and the annual camel fair. Best time to visit is during November for the Pushkar Fair.

  7. Ajmer - Known for the Ajmer Sharif Dargah, Ana Sagar Lake, and historic landmarks. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  8. Mount Abu - The only hill station in Rajasthan, Mount Abu offers cool weather, the Dilwara Temples, and Nakki Lake. Best time to visit is during the summer season (March-June).

  9. Chittorgarh - Known for the majestic Chittorgarh Fort and tales of bravery. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  10. Ranthambore - Famous for its national park, Ranthambore is a wildlife enthusiast's paradise. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  11. Alwar - Home to the Bhangarh Fort, Sariska Tiger Reserve, and the City Palace. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  12. Barmer - Known for its sand dunes, wood carvings, and folk music. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  13. Kota - Famous for its Chambal Garden, Seven Wonders Park, and educational hubs. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  14. Bundi - Known for its palaces, stepwells, and Taragarh Fort. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  15. Ranakpur - Famous for its intricately designed Jain temples. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  16. Mandawa - Known for its frescoed havelis and Rajasthani art. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  17. Sikar - Famous for its heritage havelis and historical landmarks. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  18. Shekhawati - Known for its painted havelis and cultural richness. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  19. Jhalawar - Famous for its temples, palaces, and scenic beauty. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  20. Tonk - Known for its historical monuments and the Sunheri Kothi. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).
