Top 20 Cities to See in Uttar Pradesh


  1. Varanasi - Known as the "Spiritual Capital of India", Varanasi is famous for its ghats, temples, and the Ganges. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  2. Agra - Home to the iconic Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, and Fatehpur Sikri. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  3. Lucknow - Known as the "City of Nawabs", Lucknow boasts the Bara Imambara, Chota Imambara, and Rumi Darwaza. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  4. Ayodhya - A sacred city and the birthplace of Lord Rama, famous for Ram Janmabhoomi. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  5. Mathura - Known as the birthplace of Lord Krishna, Mathura is home to temples and ghats. Best time to visit is during Janmashtami or the winter season (October-March).

  6. Prayagraj - Known for the Triveni Sangam and the Kumbh Mela. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  7. Kanpur - Famous for its Green Park, Allen Forest Zoo, and textile industry. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  8. Jhansi - Known for its historic forts and connection to Rani Laxmi Bai. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  9. Chitrakoot - A sacred site connected to Lord Rama's exile, with natural beauty. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  10. Sarnath - A significant Buddhist site where Lord Buddha gave his first sermon. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  11. Fatehpur Sikri - A UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its Mughal architecture. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  12. Vrindavan - A spiritual town associated with Lord Krishna's childhood. Best time to visit is during the Holi festival or the winter season (October-March).

  13. Meerut - Known for its role in India's freedom struggle and historical landmarks. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  14. Aligarh - Famous for the Aligarh Muslim University and historical sites. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  15. Gorakhpur - Known for the Gorakhnath Temple and its cultural heritage. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  16. Ghaziabad - A rapidly growing city with modern infrastructure and parks. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  17. Allahabad - Known for its rivers, the Sangam, and the Kumbh Mela. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  18. Barabanki - Famous for its ancient temples and cultural heritage. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  19. Noida - A modern city with malls, parks, and entertainment hubs. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  20. Rae Bareli - Known for its historical importance and architectural landmarks. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).
