Top 20 Places to Visit in Dadra and Nagar Haveli


  1. Silvassa - The capital city known for its tribal culture and scenic beauty. Best time to visit is October to March.

  2. Dudhni Lake - A serene lake ideal for boating and water sports. Best time to visit is October to March.

  3. Vanganga Lake Garden - A beautiful garden with a lake and walking paths. Best time to visit is October to March.

  4. Hirwa Van Garden - A lush green park perfect for relaxation. Best time to visit is October to March.

  5. Tribal Cultural Museum - Showcasing the rich heritage of local tribes. Best time to visit is year-round.

  6. Khanvel - A picturesque town surrounded by hills and forests. Best time to visit is October to March.

  7. Satmaliya Deer Park - A wildlife park home to deer, peacocks, and other species. Best time to visit is October to March.

  8. Swaminarayan Temple - A stunning temple known for its intricate architecture. Best time to visit is year-round.

  9. Ban Ganga Lake - A hidden gem surrounded by nature. Best time to visit is October to March.

  10. Madhuban Dam - A scenic spot for picnics and nature lovers. Best time to visit is October to March.

  11. Tadkeshwar Temple - An ancient Shiva temple surrounded by nature. Best time to visit is year-round.

  12. Vanvihar Tourist Complex - A popular spot for nature lovers and eco-tourists. Best time to visit is October to March.

  13. Bindrabin Temple - A revered temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. Best time to visit is year-round.

  14. Kauncha - A small village ideal for eco-tourism and trekking. Best time to visit is October to March.

  15. Tapovan Tourist Complex - A peaceful retreat with natural beauty. Best time to visit is October to March.

  16. Dadra Garden - A scenic garden known for its peaceful atmosphere. Best time to visit is October to March.

  17. Hirva Lake - A hidden natural attraction perfect for relaxation. Best time to visit is October to March.

  18. Mandoni Dam - A less-explored destination ideal for nature lovers. Best time to visit is October to March.

  19. Surangi River - A tranquil spot offering scenic beauty and peaceful surroundings. Best time to visit is October to March.
