Top 20 Cities to See in Jharkhand


  1. Ranchi - Known as the "City of Waterfalls," famous for Dassam Falls, Hundru Falls, and Rock Garden. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  2. Deoghar - Home to the sacred Baidyanath Temple, one of the 12 Jyotirlingas. Best time to visit is during the monsoon and winter season (July-March).

  3. Dhanbad - Known as the "Coal Capital of India," famous for Maithon Dam and Panchet Dam. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  4. Jamshedpur - Known as the "Steel City," famous for Jubilee Park, Dimna Lake, and Tata Steel Zoological Park. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  5. Hazaribagh - Known for Hazaribagh National Park and Canary Hill. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  6. Netarhat - Known as the "Queen of Chotanagpur," famous for its sunrise and sunset points. Best time to visit is during the summer and monsoon season (March-September).

  7. Palamu - Famous for Betla National Park, waterfalls, and wildlife safaris. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  8. Giridih - Known for Parasnath Hills, a prominent Jain pilgrimage site. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  9. Bokaro - Known for Bokaro Steel Plant, Jawaharlal Nehru Biological Park, and cooling ponds. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  10. Chaibasa - Famous for its natural beauty, waterfalls, and tribal culture. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  11. Godda - Known for its religious significance, including the Lalmati Durga Temple. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  12. Pakur - Famous for its stone mining and the Mahakal Temple. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  13. Simdega - Known for scenic beauty, Kelaghagh Dam, and tribal culture. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  14. Khunti - Known as the birthplace of tribal freedom fighter Birsa Munda, with waterfalls and forests. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  15. Lohardaga - Famous for its tribal culture, ancient ruins, and natural beauty. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  16. Ramgarh - Known for Patratu Valley and waterfalls. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  17. Sahibganj - Famous for the Rajmahal Hills, Moti Jharna, and historical significance. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  18. Chatra - Known for its waterfalls and wildlife, including Lawalong Wildlife Sanctuary. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  19. Dumka - Known for its religious sites, including Maluti Temples and Baba Basukinath Temple. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  20. Gumla - Famous for Anjan Dham, the birthplace of Lord Hanuman, and natural beauty. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).
