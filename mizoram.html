Top 20 Cities to See in Mizoram


  1. Aizawl - The capital city known for Mizoram State Museum, Durtlang Hills, and cultural heritage. Best time to visit is October to March.

  2. Lunglei - A scenic town offering panoramic views and trekking opportunities. Best time to visit is October to March.

  3. Champhai - Known for its vineyards, Rih Dil Lake, and Indo-Myanmar border views. Best time to visit is October to March.

  4. Serchhip - Famous for Vantawng Falls and wildlife sanctuaries. Best time to visit is October to March.

  5. Reiek - A tranquil village offering Reiek Tlang for hiking and scenic beauty. Best time to visit is October to March.

  6. Kolasib - Known for its serene landscapes and agricultural significance. Best time to visit is October to March.

  7. Thenzawl - Popular for Tam Dil Lake and handloom products. Best time to visit is October to March.

  8. Phawngpui - Also known as the Blue Mountain, it is ideal for trekking and nature exploration. Best time to visit is October to March.

  9. Hnahlan - Renowned for its grape vineyards and wineries. Best time to visit is October to March.

  10. Biate - Known for its peace and serene environment, perfect for a getaway. Best time to visit is October to March.

  11. Mamit - Home to Dampa Tiger Reserve and stunning landscapes. Best time to visit is October to March.

  12. Lawngtlai - Offers untouched natural beauty and is a gateway to Phawngpui National Park. Best time to visit is October to March.

  13. Saitual - Known for its picturesque setting and cultural richness. Best time to visit is October to March.

  14. Zawlnuam - A perfect destination for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts. Best time to visit is October to March.

  15. Ngopa - A serene town surrounded by lush greenery. Best time to visit is October to March.

  16. Chawngte - Known for its tranquil environment and cultural diversity. Best time to visit is October to March.

  17. Vairengte - Offers breathtaking views and is known for its Counter-Insurgency and Jungle Warfare School. Best time to visit is October to March.

  18. Tlabung - Known for its scenic riverine beauty. Best time to visit is October to March.

  19. Zokhawthar - A small village offering mesmerizing border views. Best time to visit is October to March.

  20. Hlimen - A peaceful destination with natural charm. Best time to visit is October to March.
