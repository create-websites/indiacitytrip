Top 20 Cities to See in Karnataka


  1. Bengaluru - Known as the "Silicon Valley of India," famous for Cubbon Park, Lalbagh Botanical Garden, and tech hubs. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-February).

  2. Mysore - Known as the "City of Palaces," famous for Mysore Palace, Chamundi Hills, and Dasara celebrations. Best time to visit is during October-February.

  3. Coorg - Known as the "Scotland of India," famous for coffee plantations, Abbey Falls, and serene landscapes. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  4. Hampi - A UNESCO World Heritage Site, famous for ancient temples, ruins, and boulder landscapes. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  5. Chikmagalur - Famous for coffee plantations, Mullayanagiri Peak, and trekking trails. Best time to visit is during September-May.

  6. Mangalore - Known for beaches, Udupi cuisine, and temples like Kadri Manjunath. Best time to visit is during October-February.

  7. Badami - Famous for rock-cut caves, Agastya Lake, and ancient temples. Best time to visit is during November-February.

  8. Bandipur - Known for Bandipur National Park, a haven for wildlife enthusiasts. Best time to visit is during October-May.

  9. Gokarna - Known for its pristine beaches like Om Beach and spiritual temples. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  10. Bijapur - Famous for Gol Gumbaz, Ibrahim Rauza, and other Islamic architectural marvels. Best time to visit is during October-February.

  11. Hubli-Dharwad - Known for its historic architecture, Unkal Lake, and Chandramouleshwara Temple. Best time to visit is during November-February.

  12. Shivamogga - Famous for Jog Falls, Agumbe, and Sharavati Valley Wildlife Sanctuary. Best time to visit is during September-February.

  13. Belur - Known for the Chennakesava Temple, a fine example of Hoysala architecture. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  14. Halebidu - Famous for its twin temples, showcasing intricate carvings. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  15. Kudremukh - Known for its lush green hills, trekking trails, and waterfalls. Best time to visit is during September-February.

  16. Udupi - Famous for Sri Krishna Temple, St. Mary's Island, and local cuisine. Best time to visit is during October-February.

  17. Murudeshwar - Home to the iconic Murudeshwar Temple and stunning beaches. Best time to visit is during October-May.

  18. Kolar - Known for its gold mines, Kotilingeshwara Temple, and scenic landscapes. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  19. Raichur - Famous for Raichur Fort, temples, and historical significance. Best time to visit is during October-March.

  20. Karwar - Known for its tranquil beaches, Kali River, and Warship Museum. Best time to visit is during October-May.
