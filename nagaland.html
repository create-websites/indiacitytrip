Top 20 Cities to See in Nagaland


  1. Kohima - The capital city known for its World War II Cemetery, Dzukou Valley, and Naga heritage. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  2. Dimapur - Famous for its ancient ruins, Kachari Rajbari, and shopping markets. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  3. Mon - Known for its unique tribal culture, Longwa Village, and the Aoling Festival. Best time to visit is during March-April.

  4. Mokokchung - A cultural hub of the Ao tribe, famous for its festivals and heritage. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  5. Wokha - Renowned for its lush landscapes, rivers, and terrace farming. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  6. Phek - Famous for Shilloi Lake, trekking routes, and natural beauty. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  7. Tuensang - Known for its ethnic diversity and traditional practices. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  8. Zunheboto - Renowned for Sumi Naga heritage and the largest Baptist church in Asia. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  9. Khonoma - Asia's first green village, famous for its eco-tourism and conservation efforts. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  10. Pfutsero - Known for being the coldest inhabited place in Nagaland, with scenic landscapes. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  11. Kiphire - Home to Mount Saramati, the highest peak in Nagaland. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  12. Peren - Known for its waterfalls, forest reserves, and the fascinating Zeliang Naga culture. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  13. Meluri - Famous for its crystal-clear waters, unique rock formations, and the Dzudu Lake. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  14. Chumukedima - Renowned for its waterfalls and panoramic views of Dimapur. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  15. Noklak - A serene destination known for its tribal traditions and handicrafts. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  16. Tseminyu - Known for its archaeological sites and historical significance. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  17. Pughoboto - Famous for its scenic beauty and peaceful environment. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  18. Chiephobozou - Known for its serene villages and agricultural practices. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  19. Medziphema - A gateway to numerous tourist spots and known for its agricultural university. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  20. Athibung - Famous for its natural beauty, rivers, and unexplored trails. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).
