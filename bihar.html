Top 20 Places to Visit in Bihar


  1. Mahabodhi Temple - UNESCO World Heritage Site where Buddha attained enlightenment. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  2. Nalanda University - Ancient center of learning and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  3. Rajgir - Known for its hot springs, Vishwa Shanti Stupa, and scenic beauty. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  4. Vaishali - A historic Buddhist site featuring ancient stupas. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  5. Kesariya Stupa - The largest stupa in India. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  6. Barabar Caves - Oldest surviving rock-cut caves in India. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  7. Patna Sahib Gurudwara - A significant Sikh pilgrimage site. Best time to visit is year-round.

  8. Vikramshila University - An ancient seat of learning. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  9. Bhagalpur - Known for its silk industry and natural beauty. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  10. Sun Temple, Deo - Ancient temple dedicated to the Sun God. Best time to visit is during the Chhath Festival (October-November).

  11. Rohtas Fort - Historic fort offering stunning views. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  12. Munger - Known for its ancient forts and Bihar School of Yoga. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  13. Kakolat Waterfall - A picturesque waterfall and picnic spot. Best time to visit is during the monsoon season (July-September).

  14. Jal Mandir - A temple surrounded by water in Pawapuri. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  15. Bodh Gaya Multimedia Museum - An interactive museum about Buddha's life. Best time to visit is year-round.

  16. Chandan Dam - A serene spot for nature lovers. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  17. Sher Shah Suri Tomb - A historical mausoleum in Sasaram. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  18. Rajendra Smriti Sangrahalaya - Museum dedicated to Dr. Rajendra Prasad. Best time to visit is year-round.

  19. Kanwar Lake - Asia's largest freshwater oxbow lake. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).

  20. Darbhanga - Known for its forts, palaces, and Mithila culture. Best time to visit is during the winter season (October-March).
